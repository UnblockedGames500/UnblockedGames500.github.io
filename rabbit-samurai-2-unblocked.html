Clicker Heroes unblocked

An original idle clicker game is called Clicker Heroes. Slay creatures, find treasure, defeat bosses, improve heroes with the gold you gather, and more as you go on an epic quest. Along with other classics like Cookie Clicker, Clicker Heroes is a game that contributed to the mainstreaming of clicker games.

To kill, click
Set out on a mission to develop into a formidable warrior. Click on each beast you see several times to kill it, then collect the gold awards that are left behind. You have a single damage number for each click you make, which you can constantly increase for more power.

Hire heroes to assist you
To defeat demons more quickly, hire several heroes. Each hero has their own DPS and occasionally distinct upgrades. The hero upgrades frequently allow for further, considerable increases in DPS. With the help of heroes, you may easily destroy hostile mobs and steal their gold. The heroes become ever more expensive, but you get the strength and strong upgrades you pay for. More heroes appear as you go, becoming your next major investment that enables you to advance into more prosperous countries.

Discover new places and defeat evildoers
Monsters are different and intriguing in each place. Move from the forest to the drylands and other places starting at the forest. There is a challenging boss battle that requires all of your strength to defeat because the opponent has extra health at the conclusion of each area. If you are unable to defeat the boss, you will return to the previous stage where you can improve your skills before fighting again.

The clicker game Clicker Heroes is a big one with lots to learn. Read the guides on the Clicker Heroes wiki to learn more about all the characters and bosses, as well as how to advance and transcend.
